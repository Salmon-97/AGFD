Efo Riro Efo Riro is life

Efo riro (Yoruba: ẹ̀fọ́ riro) is a vegetable soup and a native soup of the Yoruba people of South West Nigeria and other parts of Yorubaland.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable
  • Palmoil
  • Beaf
  • Smoke Fish
  • Cow Skin/Ponmo
  • Ofalls
  • Locust Bean
  • Pepper and Onion

How To Prepare Efo Riro

  1. Start by setting down your ingredients to make sure everything is complete.
  2. Wash your pot, the beef, offals, ponmo, fishes and pepper and set them all aside.
  3. Chop your onions and also boil your proteins all together by the side.
  4. Blend your pepper which inludes habenero pepper and scotch bonnet with plenty of onions and set it aside.
  5. Put your pot on fire, when it's dry, add Palmoil and let it hot for awhile then pour in your onion let it fry for a minute and add your grated pepper.
  6. Add the locust bean and your seasons to the frying pepper, cover it and let it for 3 minutes.
  7. Add your protein to the pepper and the stock, cover it and let it cook.
  8. While your soup is cooking, wash your vegetable thoroughly because of sand.
  9. After the soup has cooked for 10 minutes, add the washed vegetable and stir very well so everything can miss together.
  10. Cover and let it cook for another 10 minutes and your efo riro is ready.
  11. Serve while hot with your choice of swallow, mine is poundo or eba.
